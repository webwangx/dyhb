Wuhan Dingye Environmental Protection Engineering Technology Co., Ltd.


Wuhan Dingye Environmental Protection Engineering Technology Co., Ltd. is a hi-tech enterprise dedicated to the production, R&D and sales of customized sealing and high temperature insulation products.

It provides customized molding thermostatic components (mainly specially-designed removable thermal insulation jacket and thermal insulation works), all types of composite casings, protection blankets (high-temperature nanometer monet stone, carbon alloy, high-carbon fiber etc.) and such other thermal insulation products.


Our products are widely applied in food, pharmaceutical, petrochemical, tire, rubber and plastic products, chemicals and chemical engineering, thermal and nuclear power, ship-building, engineering machinery, textile, paper, metallurgical and other industries. Our clients include BAOSTEEL, ANSTEEL, Shougang Group, Wuhan Iron and Steel from the metallurgical industry and Zhuzhou Smelter Group, Jiangxi Copper, Tongling Nonferrous Metals, Daye Nonferrous Metals, Zijin Mining from the nonferrous industry.


As a fast-growing hi-tech enterprise invested by foreign capital, we’ve been rated as a gazelle company located at the Donghu High-tech Development Zone for three consecutive years. We boast the best partner for companies that are in pursuit of energy saving and industrial insulation because we aim to reduce operating costs for our clients and bring them considerable economic benefits. We make it possible for our clients to have the highest economic benefits, and allow them to harvest long-term earnings with short-term investment. Our efforts contribute to the purpose of our partners to increase income and reduce expenditure and the cause of green earth.


Currently we’ve concluded a strategic cooperation agreement valued at RMB 20 million with Wuhan University of Technology and Industrial Technology Research Institute, intending to create higher value for our clients by increasing R&D investment in non-standard customized products and localization of import products. Our on-site project departments spread all over 42 domestic cities (including Chongqing, Kunming, Nanjing, Shenyang), and we are the only one provider in this industry that has office in Canada.


As a response to the trend on Internet, we’ve built a platform - Dingye Technology for technical exchange, which brings excellent technical videos together from all kinds of industries and areas. This platform is also a platform to exchange and showcase remarkable projects, and it allows clients to promote good technical projects. On this platform, you can or have agent publish any “pain points” encountered in your work, and all pain points can be released on this platform as commercial opportunities. Dingye Technology helps you gain extra benefits by integrating resources together. It offers possibilities that resources and experience can be used to create value and benefits.

Our Company is a hi-tech enterprise dedicated to the production

Wuhan Dingye Environmental Protection Engineering Technology Co., Ltd. is a hi-tech enterprise dedicated to the production, R&D and sales of customized sealing and high temperature insulation products.

It provides customized molding thermostatic components (mainly specially-designed removable thermal insulation jacket and thermal insulation works), all types of composite casings, protection blankets (high-temperature nanometer monet stone, carbon alloy, high-carbon fiber etc.) and such other thermal insulation products.

High Density Composites
The pipeline online seal assembly (medium and low pressure) consists of three parts, namely high strength fiber, high strength filler and high performance adhesive. In repairing the defects, the filler is first used for filling and leveling up on the defective parts, and then the high density composite composed of high strength fiber cloth and adhesive is wound outside the pipeline. The high density composite has high tensile strength and elastic modulus after curing, which can effectively control the pipeline deformation at the defect area, for reinforced remediation of pipeline defects.

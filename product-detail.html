Seal up[Product name]

2019-03-14 15

The pipeline online seal assembly (medium and low pressure) consists of three parts, namely high strength fiber, high strength filler and high performance adhesive. In repairing the defects, the filler is first used for filling and leveling up on the defective parts, and then the high density composite composed of high strength fiber cloth and adhesive is wound outside the pipeline. The high density composite has high tensile strength and elastic modulus after curing, which can effectively control the pipeline deformation at the defect area, for reinforced remediation of pipeline defects.
